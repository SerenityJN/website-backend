Welcome to the SVSHS Online Application for Admission!

To proceed with your application, please do the following:

  1. Be ready to upload your 2" x 2" photo with white background and any of the following:

    • For graduates of Non-Formal Education: PEPT/ALS rating

    • For degree holders: Transcript of records
    • For cross-enrollees: Permit to cross-enroll

  3. Make sure to COPY/TAKE A SCREENSHOT of your reference number. You’ll use this later.

Data Privacy Notice and Consent Form

Southville 8B Senior High School values and respects your right to privacy and ensures that all personal information you provide will be processed in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173).

1. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal data will be collected, used, and stored for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate your eligibility for admission and enrollment.
  • To maintain accurate student records for administrative and academic purposes.
  • To comply with reporting requirements to the Department of Education and other authorized agencies.
  • To communicate school-related updates, events, and announcements.

2. Types of Personal Data Collected

The following information may be collected from you:

  • Personal details (name, age, sex, nationality, religion, etc.)
  • Contact information (email, address, phone number)
  • Educational background (previous schools, grades, etc.)
  • Documents (birth certificate, Form 137, good moral certificate)

3. Data Protection and Security

All information will be stored securely in the school’s student database and will only be accessed by authorized personnel. The school implements appropriate physical, technical, and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Retention and Sharing

Your personal data will be retained for as long as necessary for the fulfillment of the above-stated purposes or as required by law. Sharing of data will only occur with your consent or when mandated by regulatory authorities.

By clicking “Proceed to Admission Portal,” I hereby give my consent to Southville 8B Senior High School to collect, process, and store my personal data for legitimate school-related purposes. I confirm that I have read and understood this Data Privacy Notice.
